## Attribution Requirement (CERN-OHL-W v2, Section 4.1)

Any product which incorporates or is based on SPECTER firmware must comply with the following:

### 1. Source Location

The source location of this firmware must be communicated to any recipient of a product using it:

> https://github.com/KjernNo/specter

This must appear in at least one of: the product itself, its packaging, or its accompanying documentation.

### 2. Boot Screen Branding (Products with a Display)

Any product that uses SPECTER firmware and includes a display must show text equivalent to one of the following on the boot screen:

> "SPECTER, by Kjern"
> "Powered with SPECTER, by Kjern"
> or equivalent wording that clearly identifies SPECTER and Kjern.no as the origin of the firmware.

**Requirements:**
- The text must be visible to the end user during the boot sequence
- The text must be clearly legible and reasonably prominent, it may not be so small or brief that a typical user would not notice it
- The text may be positioned anywhere on the display at the product manufacturer's discretion
- Additional branding, product names, or logos may be shown alongside or before this text
- The text may not be removed, hidden, or obscured

**This attribution must not imply, suggest, or create the impression that Kjern.no or SPECTER endorses, sponsors, or is affiliated with your product or organisation. The attribution is credit only.**

**Acceptable examples:**
- "SPECTER, by Kjern" shown in a corner while the manufacturer's branding is the main focus
- The manufacturer's logo shown first, followed by "Powered with SPECTER, by Kjern" beneath
- "Built on SPECTER by Kjern.no" shown anywhere on the boot screen in a readable size

**Not acceptable:**
- Removing the text entirely
- Replacing it with only your own branding
- Making the text so small it cannot reasonably be read
- Displaying it so briefly it cannot reasonably be noticed
- Wording it in a way that implies Kjern.no or SPECTER endorses or sponsors your product

### 3. Copyright Notices in Source Code

All copyright notices and SPDX licence identifiers present in the source files must be retained in any distribution of the source, modified or unmodified, as required by CERN-OHL-W v2 sections 3.1 and 3.3(a).
