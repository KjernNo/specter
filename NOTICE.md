## Attribution Requirement (CERN-OHL-W v2, Section 4.1)

Any product which incorporates or is based on SPECTER firmware must comply with the following notice:

### 1. Source Location
The source location of this firmware must be communicated to any recipient of a product using it:

> https://github.com/KjernNo/specter

This must appear in at least one of: the product itself, its packaging, or its accompanying documentation.

### 2. Boot Screen Branding (Display Products)
Any product that uses SPECTER firmware and includes a display must show the following text on the boot screen:

> SPECTER
> by Kjern

**Requirements:**
- The text must be visible to the end user during the boot sequence
- The text must be clearly legible and reasonably prominent — it may not be made so small or brief that a typical user would not notice it
- The text may be repositioned anywhere on the display at the product manufacturer's discretion
- Additional branding, logos, or product names may be shown alongside or before this text
- The text may not be removed, hidden, or obscured

**Acceptable examples:**
- Showing "SPECTER / by Kjern" in a corner of the boot screen while the manufacturer's own branding is prominent
- Showing the manufacturer's logo first, followed by "SPECTER by Kjern" beneath it
- Showing "Powered by SPECTER — by Kjern" in any position on the boot screen

**Not acceptable:**
- Removing the text entirely
- Replacing it with only the manufacturer's own branding
- Displaying it in a font so small it cannot be reasonably read
- Displaying it for so short a time it cannot be reasonably noticed

### 3. Copyright Notices
All copyright notices and SPDX licence identifiers present in the source files must be retained in any distribution of the source code, modified or unmodified, as required by CERN-OHL-W v2 sections 3.1 and 3.3(a).
